Projects

Thesis

Ph.D. Thesis

In this thesis I studied the synaptic transmission in autonomic ganglia and in adrenal medulla in diabetes. I find that the synaptic transmission is defective in the sympathetic ganglia and the adrenal due to inactivation of postsynaptic nicotinic receptors by reactive oxygen species in diabetes. I show that parasympathetic ganglion, on the other hand, has normal synaptic transmission in diabetes, and its neurons are resistant to elevations of reactive oxygen species.

Comprehensive Exam

Written Proposal

In this project I studied the role of innate immune response mediated by macrophages in Tuberculosis. Specifically, I propose to look at the role of reactive oxygen species in amplifying Toll-like receptor signalling.
